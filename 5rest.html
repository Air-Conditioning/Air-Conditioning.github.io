Resting to recover energy

You walk to the nearest rock that has a flat enough surface to sit comfortably on. You lean back into a position you decide is the most comfortable and wait…

Sitting on this rock, you feel a bit calmer and level-headed. You continue to wait and rest…

And wait…

And wait…

And wait…

An unknown amount of time passes before you sigh to yourself. At least you do feel a bit more rested.

The caw of a raven startles you to your feet.

—> What was that?
