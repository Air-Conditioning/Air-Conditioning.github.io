Consume the stew

Having no other choice, you ate the contents of the stew and sipped the broth. It was delicious, but you couldn’t shake the feeling of unease that washed over you as you ate quietly. Looking over at the old woman, she watched you with interest and smiled.

Your vision began to blur. That’s not good…

Feeling sick, you made a move to stand, but your legs felt like lead and refused to work. You could only look at the woman helplessly as she continued to watch you, her expression unchanging. Your eyes drifted shut and you fell unconscious.

You startled awake and in a cold sweat, eyes snapping open. You found yourself in your room, in the comfort of your bed. Looking down at your clothes, you noticed they were clean, nothing like the state they were in in your… dream? Feeling rattled, you dragged yourself out of bed and to the bathroom. Looking into the mirror, you notice something different. Although you appeared the same, you found small black feathers sprouting along your jaw and at your collarbone.

You made it out alive…?
