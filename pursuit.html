The Pursuit Animatic (Spring 2022)

This animatic is focused on a story surrounding my original characters Avichrys, Kheinyx, and Meep. To summarize, this animatic starts with Avichrys, a product of animal experimentation, finding a way to escape her enclosure in the lab. Shortly after, the lab's security, Kheinyx, gives chase. The pursuit of Kheinyx is meant to be misleading as the perspective comes from Avichrys, who is being chased, when in the end, Kheinyx is only acting to retrieve the escapee. This animatic was drawn in Krita and compiled with Photoshop.
