Nature's Natural Glitches (Fall 2024)

This is my glitch art series of different mountain ranges. I chose to glitch mountains because I consider them to be majestic landforms. I also find it poetic how mountains and other types of ecosystems are created and built upon through chance, similarly to glitches. Sometimes, things happen purely by chance, and I think that's what makes nature beautiful.
