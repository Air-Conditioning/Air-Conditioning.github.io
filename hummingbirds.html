Bouncy Birds (Spring 2025)

Bouncy Birds is a sketch where I illustrated hummingbirds on P5js as a class to create two with different plumage. I included a flower for one on the birds to drink nectar from while the other is coded with bounce and jitter to excitedly bounce around the screen.
