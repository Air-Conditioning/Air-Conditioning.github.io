Donut Shop Animatic (Fall 2023)

I was presented with the challenge to storyboard and form an animatic for a script I was given titled "The Donut Shop", and this animatic is the result. I hadn't known at the time, but the original scene is from the movie Boogie Nights. The voices for the man in the suit, the donut boy, and the robber are voiced by my friends Caleb, Sophie, and Alex respectively. The drawings were created in Krita and compiled with Photoshop.
