About Me

My name is Akari Che, and I am a San Jose State University student majoring in Digital Media Arts with a focus on creative coding. Growing up alongside the development of the internet, I was raised on digital media like video games and animation, and I hold a fondness for these art forms. The feeling of being completely immersed in a form of media because of its captivating way of telling a story or creating a world which the viewer can lose themselves in is what motivates me to create the same. I believe learning different skills in Digital Media Arts would allow me to make similar media, which is why I strive to learn all kinds of new skill from this program. I currently have some experience in animation, video editing, 3d modeling, and creative coding.
