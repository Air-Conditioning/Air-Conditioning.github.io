A Fishy Home (Spring 2025)

A Fishy Home is an interactive work of a pet fish within an aquarium. I created this code in P5js so that you could guide the fish on a grid to explore its spacious home. When you click the mouse button, you can change the goldfish into a jellyfish. Hovering your mouse over the axolotl changes the cursor into a hand which allows you to pet it.

Keybinds:

[W, A, S, D] - Movement

[t/T] - "Fish!" Text

[Click Mouse] - Change fish
