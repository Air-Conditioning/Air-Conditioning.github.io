Minecraft Among Us (Fall 2024)

I chose to build the character from the game Among Us when challenged to build something significant to internet culture. Among Us is culturally significant to the internet because of how undeniably popular it became, gaining virility through memes. I believe part of why it grew so popular is because of how the game's simplicity (in relation to other games) is what made it accessible to audiences of all ages, especially younger audiences. That combined with its cute and simple design is probably what allowed it to spread through the internet like wildfire. Regardless of how we feel now that the trend has been exhausted, Among Us has left its mark on online culture.
