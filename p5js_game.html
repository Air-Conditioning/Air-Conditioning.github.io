Duck Hunt Recreation (Fall 2024)

Duck Hunt Recreation is my attempt at recreating the Duck Hunt game on P5js. The goal of the game is to hover over the duck with your cursor to increase your score. You can progress the level every 10 points. The game goes on for 12 levels and the duck gets progressively harder to locate with each one. Although I've never played Duck Hunt, I like its straight-forward concept and believed I could remake it with P5js.
